Job Description

As a Technical Lead at our company, you will be at the forefront of driving technical excellence and leading a team of talented engineers. Your role will encompass a mix of technical expertise, team management, and strategic decision-making. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to ensure the successful delivery of complex projects while maintaining a high standard of code quality and performance.

The Role

Responsibilities:

  • Provide technical leadership and guidance to a team of developers, ensuring efficient project execution and adherence to best practices.
  • Architect and design robust, scalable, and maintainable software solutions that align with business requirements and industry standards.
  • Collaborate with product managers and stakeholders to define project scope, timelines, and deliverables, and actively participate in project planning and estimation.
  • Conduct code reviews and provide constructive feedback to team members, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and knowledge sharing.
  • Stay up to date with the latest technologies, frameworks, and industry trends, and proactively identify opportunities to enhance the team's technical capabilities.
  • Troubleshoot complex technical issues, identify root causes, and implement effective solutions, ensuring the stability and performance of software applications.
  • Mentor and coach team members, promoting their professional growth and fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment.

    • Requirements:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Technical Lead or similar role, demonstrating strong leadership skills and the ability to lead and motivate a team.
  • In-depth knowledge of software development principles, including software architecture, design patterns, and coding standards.
  • Proficiency in multiple programming languages and technologies commonly used in the organization's tech stack.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to analyze complex technical challenges and propose effective solutions.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams and stakeholders.
  • Experience with Agile development methodologies and tools.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms, such as AWS or Azure, and modern DevOps practices is a plus.
